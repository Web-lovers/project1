Doctor Abdul Qadeer

1936 - 2021

Missile Man Of Pakistan

Abdul Qadeer Khan was born on 1 April 1936, in Bhopal, a city then in the erstwhile British Indian princely state of Bhopal State, and now the capital city of Madhya Pradesh. He was a Muhajir of Urdu-speaking Pashtun origin.[16][17][2][5][18] His maternal ancestors hailed from the Tirah Valley (now in the Khyber District of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan) while from his paternal side, an ancestor of his was an Uzbek soldier who came to India with Muhammad of Ghor, the 12th century conqueror, the reason why years later he would name his ballistic missiles as Ghauri.[19] In 1994-1995, Khan would build a mausoleum for Ghori situated at around 80 kilometres from Islamabad.[20]
